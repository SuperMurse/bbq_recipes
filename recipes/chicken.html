Want to cook the juiciest chicken with littl prep and work? Only several steps away from this succulent poultry. Season, Smoke, Slice. It is my favorite way to cook chicken and is always a hit at the party!

Ingredients:

  • Whole Chicken
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Temperature probe

Steps:

  1. Preheat smoker to 375F
  2. Season the entire chicken with your seasoning of choice.
  3. Place chicken on smoker with breast meat up.
  4. Insert temperature probe into middle of breas meat.
  5. Once internal temp reaches 165F, pull off chicken to rest for about 15-20 minutes.
  6. Slice chicken to your preference and enjoy!
