Stormy's Pork Butt

Home

Stormy's pulled pork recipe will have your friends and family coming back for seconds and even thirds. Slow smoked over 8 hours to develope a delicious crunchy bark; followed by 2-3 hours wrapped in foil to finish to a final temp of 205f with juices that are held in tight to provide the juicest and most flavorful bite!

Ingredients:

  • Pork Shoulder (bone in)
  • Temp probe
  • Favorite bbq rub
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Foil
  • 1 stick of salted butter
  • 2 cups of favorite bbq sauce
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Steps:

    Step 1: Season pork butt liberally with rub. I use a mustard base to help the rub stick
    Step 2: While pork butt soaks in sesoning; Pre-heat smoker to 225f.
    Step 3: Place pork butt onto smoker with temp probe in place. Spritz with apple cider vinegar throughout cook.
    Step 4: Once pork butt reaches interal temp of around 165, it will hit the "stall." Pull off pork butt and prepare to wrap.
    Step 5: Wrap pork butt in foil along with butter, bbq sauce and brown sugar.
    Step 6: Place wrapped pork butt back onto grill with temp probe in place.
    Step 7: Once internal temp reaches exactly 205f, pull off smoker and allow to rest for at least 1 hour.
    Step 8: Shred pork with two forks and mix well with juices.
    Step 9: ENJOY!
